The Perfect Tomato Salad!

A sweet and sour salad

This summery cherry tomato salad with pine nuts, olives, and a homemade vinaigrette is colorful and delicious. This recipe was passed on by a friend and has been passed on to many more friends! Always an excellent choice when entertaining for dinner.

Ingredients

  • 40 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup pitted and sliced green olives
  • 1 cup pitted and sliced green olives
  • 2 green onions, minced
  • 3 ounces pine nuts
  • Â½ cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and pepper to taste

The Steps:

  1. Step 1:

    In a big bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, green olives, back olives, and spring onion.

  2. Step 2:

    In a dry skillet, toast pine nuts over medium heat until golden brown, turning frequently. Stir into tomato mixture.

  3. Step 3:

    In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, red wine vinegar, sugar, and oregano. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Pour over salad, and gently stir to coat. Chill for 1 hour.
